1. Title and Topic of My Site

Title: The Journey to Emotional Strength

Topic: My website will focus on emotional well-being, confidence building, and self-improvement for young adults.

Why I Chose This Topic

I chose this topic because emotional strength, confidence, and self-love are things I personally care about and continue to work on. I want to create a site that inspires others who struggle with fear, doubt, or insecurity to believe in themselves. This topic is meaningful to me, and building a website about it will help me grow while sharing something uplifting and honor code appropriate.
