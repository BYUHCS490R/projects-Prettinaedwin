Healthy Relationships

Strong relationships are built on trust, respect, and understanding.

Setting Boundaries

  • Clearly communicate your needs
  • Respect your own time and space
  • Learn to say no

Supportive Relationships

  • Mutual respect
  • Encouragement instead of criticism
  • Safe to express emotions

Avoiding Toxic Behavior

  • Recognize manipulation
  • Limit energy-draining interactions
  • Seek help if needed
