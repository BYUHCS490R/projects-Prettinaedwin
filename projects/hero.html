J.K. Rowling: My Book Hero

Interests Page | Hero Page

One of the authors who inspires me the most is J.K. Rowling. Her story has always amazed me she faced so many struggles before her books were finally published, yet she never gave up on her dream. I admire the way she created the Harry Potter world, because it feels alive and full of lessons about courage, friendship, and hope. Her journey shows me that creativity and persistence can change everything.

Famous Series
Harry Potter (7 books, published between 1997 and 2007)
Awards
Winner of many honors, including British Book Awards and the Hugo Award
Other Works
The Casual Vacancy and the Cormoran Strike detective series (under the name Robert Galbraith)
