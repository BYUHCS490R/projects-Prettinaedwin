Books: My Favorite Escape

Interests Page | Hero Page

I’ve always loved reading. For me, books are like little doors into another world. Sometimes they let me step into magical adventures, and other times they help me see life from someone else’s perspective. Whenever I feel stressed or overwhelmed, picking up a book gives me comfort and calm.

What I enjoy most about books is the way they stay with you. A good story doesn’t end when you close the last page—it lingers in your thoughts and becomes a part of you. Reading has taught me to dream bigger, to think deeper, and to believe that imagination is powerful.
